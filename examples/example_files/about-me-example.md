## Style
- Data-driven but trust intuition

- Users first, always
- Ship fast, learn faster
- Perfect is the enemy of good

## Current Context
- Growth-stage startup, 50 people
- Leading mobile product line
- This quarter: Improve retention by 15%

- Technical depth (former engineer)
- User research and synthesis
- Cross-functional collaboration

## Growth Areas
- Enterprise sales cycles
- Pricing and packaging

---

## Tips

- Start simple - even a few bullets help AI understand you
- Update quarterly - keep it current with your evolving context
- Be honest - helps AI give better advice
- Include examples - "I prefer X style (example: ...)"
- Reference other docs - link to your frameworks, processes

---

**Remember**: This is for you and AI. Be authentic about how you work and what you need.
