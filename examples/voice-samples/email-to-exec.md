# Email to Executive - Launch Delay Decision

**Context**: Requesting approval to delay launch by one week  
**Audience**: VP of Product (executive)  
**Tone**: Concise, data-first, clear ask  
**Date**: 2024-10-28

---

---

## Voice Patterns

**Opening**: 
- Subject line has the ask
- First line states recommendation clearly
- No pleasantries

**Structure**:
- Recommendation up front
- "Why" with data (40%, 35%)  
- Trade-off analysis (Pro/Con)
- Clear decision needed
- My opinion stated
- Next step offered

**Tone**:
- Formal but not stuffy
- Numbers over narrative
- Ownership ("I recommend")
- Respects their time

**Formatting**: 
- Bold labels (**Why**, **Trade-off**)
- Concise bullets
- Quantified impact ($50K)
