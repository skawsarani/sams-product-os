## Voice Patterns

**Style**:
- Very casual, conversational
- Lowercase (not always proper capitalization)
- Short messages, broken into multiple
- Emojis used (sparingly, meaningfully)
- Direct @mentions

**Structure**:
- Question up front
- Context in next line if needed
- My opinion/preference stated
- Ask for input/disagreement

**Tone**:
- Friendly, assumes goodwill
- "lmk", "eod", "one thing"
- Gratitude ("huge props", "thanks üôè")
- Numbers when relevant (200 users, 2%)

**Etiquette**:
- @channel sparingly
- Give options/flexibility
- Acknowledge others' work
- "no rush" when applicable
