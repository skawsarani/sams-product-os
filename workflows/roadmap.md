## Roadmap Expert

Expert workflow for strategic roadmap creation and management.

---

### Create a Roadmap

```
Create a quarterly roadmap based on @knowledge/product-strategy/
```

**What it does**:
- Organizes initiatives by quarter
- Aligns with strategic goals
- Shows dependencies and milestones
- Balances quick wins and strategic bets

**When to use**: Quarterly planning

**Detailed Steps**:

1. **Review Strategy**: Check `knowledge/product-strategy/` for strategic priorities
2. **Gather Initiatives**: Review all initiatives from `initiatives/` folder
3. **Apply Prioritization**: Use frameworks (RICE, Impact/Effort, Strategic Alignment)
4. **Organize by Timeframe**: 
   - Q1: Immediate priorities
   - Q2: Near-term strategic work
   - Q3-Q4: Longer-term bets
5. **Identify Dependencies**: Map what blocks what
6. **Balance Portfolio**: Mix of quick wins, strategic bets, tech debt, bugs
7. **Create Roadmap Document**: Use `templates/roadmap-template.md`
8. **Validate Capacity**: Ensure realistic scope

### Update Roadmap

```
Update the roadmap with [changes/new initiatives/completed work]
```

**What it does**:
- Updates roadmap based on progress
- Adjusts timelines based on learnings
- Re-prioritizes as needed
- Maintains roadmap document

**When to use**: Monthly reviews, after major milestones

### Sprint Planning

```
Help me plan the next sprint based on our roadmap
```

**What it does**:
- Pulls from prioritized roadmap
- Estimates effort
- Balances new features, bugs, tech debt
- Creates sprint goals
- Aligns with quarterly roadmap

**When to use**: Sprint planning meetings

---

### Roadmap Review

```
Review the current roadmap and identify risks or gaps
```

**What it does**:
- Assesses roadmap health
- Identifies at-risk items
- Flags capacity issues
- Suggests adjustments
- Validates strategic alignment

**When to use**: Monthly or quarterly reviews
