## Competitor Research Expert

Expert workflow for competitor research. Can request latest updates or news about competition, and track changes from documented knowledge records.

---

### Competitive Analysis

```
Compare our approach to [feature] with competitors in @knowledge/references/
```

**What it does**:
- Creates comparison table
- Identifies differentiators
- Highlights gaps and opportunities
- Strategic recommendations
- References competitor info from `knowledge/references/`

**When to use**: Feature planning, positioning

---

### Track Competitor Changes

```
What has changed with [competitor] since we last documented them?
```

**What it does**:
- Reviews competitor documentation in `knowledge/references/`
- Identifies what we know vs. what's new
- Highlights changes in features, pricing, positioning
- Updates competitor records
- Flags significant changes

**When to use**: Regular competitor monitoring

**Detailed Steps**:

1. **Review Existing Records**: Check `knowledge/references/` for competitor docs
2. **Identify Last Update**: When was competitor info last documented?
3. **Research Changes**: What's new since then?
4. **Compare**: Old vs. new information
5. **Update Records**: Update competitor documentation
6. **Flag Changes**: Highlight significant changes

---

### Request Competitor Updates

```
Get the latest updates on [competitor] and update our records
```

**What it does**:
- Researches latest competitor information
- Updates competitor documentation in `knowledge/references/`
- Highlights new features, changes, announcements
- Compares to our product
- Identifies threats and opportunities

**When to use**: When you need fresh competitor intelligence

---

### Competitive Feature Comparison

```
Compare [feature] across competitors: [competitor A], [competitor B], [competitor C]
```

**What it does**:
- Creates detailed feature comparison table
- Scores each competitor on feature
- Identifies best-in-class approaches
- Highlights gaps in our offering
- Provides strategic recommendations

**When to use**: Feature planning, competitive positioning

---

### Monitor Competitor Landscape

```
Review our competitor landscape and identify any gaps in our knowledge
```

**What it does**:
- Reviews all competitor records in `knowledge/references/`
- Identifies outdated information
- Flags missing competitor data
- Suggests research priorities
- Creates competitor monitoring plan

**When to use**: Quarterly competitive review

---

### Document Competitor Information

```
Document information about [competitor]: [details]
```

**What it does**:
- Creates or updates competitor record
- Organizes information (features, pricing, positioning, strengths, weaknesses)
- Saves to `knowledge/references/`
- Tags with date and source
- Links to related initiatives or features

**When to use**: After competitor research or news

---

### Competitive Positioning Analysis

```
Analyze our competitive positioning vs [competitors] for [market/feature]
```

**What it does**:
- Compares positioning across competitors
- Identifies differentiation opportunities
- Highlights positioning gaps
- Suggests positioning improvements
- References product strategy for alignment

**When to use**: Positioning strategy, messaging development
